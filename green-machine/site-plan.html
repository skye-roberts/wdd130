Our Story

The Roberts family founded Green Machine in 2023, after being faced with a rarity of healthy food options in their town. The company’s main purpose is to change lives and communities from the inside out – by feeding soul as well as the body. We strive to connect people to others in their community through group cooking classes and shared seating options, and to connect people to themselves by helping them feel better and more energized through healthy, local food.

Our Philosophy

Sweeten with Fruit
No refined sugars
We’ve found that fruits are the perfect alternative to refined sugars. We will always sweeten with fruit first, and occasionally we’ll supplement with natural sweeteners like monk fruit and stevia.

Focus on the Nutrients
No more calorie counting
We strive to shift the focus in familiar diets from calorie-counting to nutrient consciousness. Calories are just one attribute of food. Nutrients involve so much more.

Made from Scratch
Every day with love
Everything we serve is made from scratch, with love, everyday. This way, we don’t need to rely on canned food or added preservatives. You know exactly what you’re getting.

Cooking Classes

We believe that good food and good conversations heal the soul, which is why we began our cooking classes. These classes provide an opportunity for the community to spend time learning and growing together, to meet their neighbors and bring their families with them. Come hungry, we always eat what we make as a group.

Brunches and Beyond
o What: Learn the art of the perfect brunch – learn simple, healthy breakfast and lunch recipes that will always impress a crowd.
o When: 1st Saturday 9:30am
o Cost: $30 per person (includes all ingredients)

(Double) Date Night
o What: Reserve ahead of time, room for 4 guests only. Experience cooking in a restaurant kitchen, learn how the chefs are making the special dish for that night’s menu, and prepare your own meal alongside them.
o When: 2nd and 4th Friday 6pm
o Cost: $75 per person (includes all ingredients)

Community Cooking
o What: Learn budget-friendly recipes that you can size up to feed any amount of people. Walk-in’s welcome, we aim to gather the whole community!
o When: 1st Monday 5:30pm
o Cost: $15/adult $10/child (includes all ingredients)

Summer Camps
We run a series of summer camps for ages 7-17, contact us for the specifics!

Private Classes
Want to prepare a special dinner for someone, uncomfortable in group settings, or have specific cooking skills/recipes you want to learn? Contact us to set up private cooking lessons!
o Cost: $25/hr, ingredient pricing varies depending on chosen recipes
